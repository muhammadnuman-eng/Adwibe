How soon will I see results from social media ads?

You'll typically start seeing data and initial results within the first 7-14 days of launching your campaign. This includes metrics like reach, engagement, and click-through rates. However, meaningful results like consistent sales or lead generation usually take 30-60 days as we gather data and optimize your campaigns. Think of it like planting a garden — you'll see sprouts quickly, but the real harvest takes a little time and care.

How much should I spend on social media advertising?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer, but here's a simple guideline: start with at least $500-$1,000 per month for testing if you're a small business. This gives us enough data to understand what works. As we identify winning strategies, we recommend scaling up to $2,500-$5,000+ monthly. The key is starting small, learning fast, and then investing more in what's actually bringing you customers — not just likes.

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

You don't need to be everywhere — just where your customers actually hang out. Here's a quick breakdown:

Facebook & Instagram: Great for almost any business, especially B2C.
LinkedIn: Perfect if you sell to other businesses (B2B).
TikTok: Ideal if your audience is under 35 and you can create fun, authentic content.
Pinterest: Excellent for lifestyle, fashion, home decor, and DIY brands.

We'll analyze your specific audience and recommend the platforms that make the most sense for your goals.

What's included in your social media management service?

Everything you need to grow on social media without the headache. This includes:

• Strategy development (who to target and what to say)
• Content creation (posts, stories, reels, and graphics)
• Daily engagement with your audience
• Paid ad management and optimization
• Monthly performance reports in plain English
• Regular strategy calls to review progress

Basically, we handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on running your business.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No locked-in contracts here. We work on a simple month-to-month basis. Why? Because we believe our results should earn your business every single month. Social media success takes consistency, and we typically recommend giving any strategy at least 90 days to show meaningful results. But if you ever feel it's not the right fit, you can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice — no hard feelings, no hidden fees.

How is this different from me just posting on my own?

Great question! Posting on your own is free, but it's like fishing with a single line from the shore. Working with us is like having a full crew with sonar equipment and the right bait for every fish.

We bring:
• Professional strategy based on real data
• High-quality creative that stops the scroll
• Advanced targeting so your content reaches the right people
• Constant optimization based on performance
• Time savings so you can focus on what you do best

Most of our clients find they actually save money by having experts handle this while they focus on their zone of genius.
