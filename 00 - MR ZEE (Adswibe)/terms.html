Welcome to Adswibe®! These Terms & Conditions outline the foundation of our partnership. By working with us, you're agreeing to a transparent, professional relationship built on mutual respect and clear expectations. We believe in keeping things simple, fair, and focused on your growth.

1. Our Services

Adswibe® provides comprehensive social media marketing solutions including but not limited to:

  • Paid advertising management across Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, Google, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Snapchat
  • Full-service social media management including content creation, scheduling, and community engagement
  • Lead generation campaigns and funnel optimization
  • Content marketing strategy and execution
  • Conversion rate optimization (CRO) and landing page optimization

Specific deliverables, timelines, and scope are detailed in individual service agreements or proposals signed by both parties.

2. Client Responsibilities

For us to deliver exceptional results, we need your partnership. As our client, you agree to:

  • Provide timely access to necessary accounts (ad accounts, business managers, analytics platforms)
  • Share accurate information about your business, target audience, and campaign goals
  • Review and approve content, creatives, and strategies within agreed timeframes
  • Maintain legal compliance of all products, services, and claims made in your advertising
  • Notify us promptly of any significant business changes that may affect campaign performance

3. Payment & Billing

Our payment terms are designed to be straightforward and predictable:

  • Monthly management fees are billed at the beginning of each service period
  • Ad spend is billed separately and must be maintained in your ad accounts directly or through our agreed arrangement
  • Invoices are due within 7 days of receipt unless otherwise specified in your agreement
  • Late payments may result in campaign pause until accounts are brought current
  • We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and selected digital payment methods

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with our strategy or initial performance within the first 30 days, we'll refund your management fee — no questions asked. Ad spend is non-refundable as it's paid directly to platforms.

4. Performance & Results

We're committed to driving measurable growth for your brand. However, social media advertising involves many variables beyond our control:

  • We provide transparent reporting and data-driven optimization
  • While we aim for exceptional results, we cannot guarantee specific metrics (ROAS, CPA, reach) due to market conditions, competition, and platform algorithm changes
  • Our strategies are based on industry best practices and continuous testing
  • We adjust campaigns proactively based on performance data

What we can guarantee: Professional management, regular communication, and our full commitment to your success.
